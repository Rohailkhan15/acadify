What is This?

Acadify is a free, fast aggregate calculator for students applying to Pakistani universities. Every year, thousands of students struggle with the same problem: they don't know their aggregate, can't find the formula, or make calculation errors. This website solves that in one place.

verified

Why Trust Us?

menu_book
Formulas from Official Prospectuses

Every weightage formula is taken directly from the official university prospectus or admission policy. We don't guess — we verify. If a university changes its formula, it gets updated here.

calculate
Transparent Calculation

The formula is always shown next to the calculator. You can see exactly what weightage each source carries before you even type a number. No black-box magic.

lock
No Account, No Data Collection

You don't need to sign up or log in. We don't store your marks, your name, or anything about you. Everything runs in your browser and disappears when you close the tab.

devices
Works on Mobile

Designed to work on your phone, not just a laptop. Whether you're checking your aggregate from a results SMS or sitting in an office filling out a form, this works.

warning

Be Aware

— University formulas can change every year. Always cross-check with the official admission prospectus.

— Not all Pakistani universities are supported yet. We're adding more over time.

— This tool calculates aggregate only. It doesn't account for extra marks, quota seats, or other admission factors that some universities apply.

— Results shown here are for reference. Official merit lists are determined by the universities themselves.

lightbulb

The Idea

Aggregate formulas are scattered across university websites, Facebook groups, and WhatsApp chains. Students spend time searching instead of calculating. The vision for this site is simple: one place where any Pakistani student can check their aggregate for any university — quickly, accurately, and without confusion. We're still building toward that goal, starting with the most-applied-to universities first.
